# Color Palette Options - Job Seeker Assistant

## Requirements from Project
- Professional but approachable (not corporate stuffy)
- Confidence-building through clear feedback
- Color-coded skill matching: green (matched), red (missing), amber (partial)
- Clean and modern aesthetic


## Warm Slate (Approachable & Friendly)

### Base Colors
- **Background**: `stone-50` (#fafaf9) - Warm neutral
- **Surface**: `white` (#ffffff)
- **Border**: `stone-200` (#e7e5e4)

### Primary Action
- **Primary**: `teal-600` (#0d9488) - Balanced, growth-oriented
- **Primary Hover**: `teal-700` (#0f766e)
- **Primary Light**: `teal-50` (#f0fdfa)

### Feedback Colors
- **Success (Matched)**: `emerald-600` (#059669) - Natural, positive
- **Success Light**: `emerald-50` (#ecfdf5)
- **Danger (Missing)**: `red-600` (#dc2626) - Direct, clear
- **Danger Light**: `red-50` (#fef2f2)
- **Warning (Partial)**: `amber-600` (#d97706) - Warm, helpful
- **Warning Light**: `amber-50` (#fffbeb)

### Text
- **Primary Text**: `stone-900` (#1c1917)
- **Secondary Text**: `stone-600` (#57534e)
- **Muted Text**: `stone-400` (#a8a29e)

### Accent (for AI chat)
- **AI Assistant**: `cyan-600` (#0891b2) - Helpful, approachable
- **AI Light**: `cyan-50` (#ecfeff)

**Vibe**: Warm, approachable, human. Good for reducing job search anxiety.
